## Website Direction

For design and product intent, read [website-spec.md](website-spec.md) before making meaningful UI changes.

Short version:

- The site should feel calm, warm, spacious, and professional.
- Avoid flashy, high-contrast, overly animated, or decorative filler UI.
- Prefer dark charcoal/graphite surfaces with soft off-white text and a muted amber/copper accent.
- Use subtle contrast, restrained glow, and gentle motion instead of heavy borders or loud effects.
- Favor clean layouts, moderate content width, real images/media, and reusable components that preserve the site's quiet tone.
